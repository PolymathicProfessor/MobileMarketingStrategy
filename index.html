Key Takeaways for 2025 Discussion

1. AI is No Longer Optional

Generative AI and machine learning are fundamentally transforming mobile marketing. Organizations not leveraging AI for personalization, content creation, and customer service will lose competitive advantage.

2. Video Dominates Mobile Consumption

75% of mobile data is video. Short-form vertical video (TikTok/Reels format) must be central to your strategy, not an afterthought.

3. Social Commerce is Where Shopping Happens

Users expect to shop directly within apps and social platforms. Integrate shoppable content, live shopping, and one-tap checkout.

4. Cross-Device Experience is Expected

Users own 3.7+ connected devices and expect seamless handoff. Design for the ecosystem, not individual devices.

5. Retention is the Real Challenge

With 75-80% uninstalling within 30 days and users regularly using only 9 of 80+ apps, your app must provide compelling daily value.

6. Privacy Compliance Shapes Strategy

Apple's ATT and privacy regulations have fundamentally changed targeting. Build first-party data strategies and transparent privacy practices.
